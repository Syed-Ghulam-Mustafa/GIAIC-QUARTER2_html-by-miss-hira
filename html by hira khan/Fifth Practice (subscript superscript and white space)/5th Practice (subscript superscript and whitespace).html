Subscript and Superscript

Subscript wo hota hai jo english alphabet k andar kuch part choty size mein ho kr nichy ko likha hota hai for example H2SO4 ko hum is tarah likhty hain H2SO4. is mein '2' aur '4' pr subscript ka tag laga hua hai.

Aur superscript wo hota hai jo english alphabet k andar kuch part choty size ka ho kr upar ko likha hota hai for example 1st and 2nd ko hum is tarah likhty hain 1st and 2nd pr superscript ka tag laga hua hai.
